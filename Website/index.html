Services offered by SquidNET

SquidNET Discord Bot

Invite the SquidNET Discord bot to your Discord server. The SquidNET Discord bot can post map rotations and weapon information. Additionally, you can connect your Twitter account to quickly repost your latest Spaltoon tweets from Discord.

SquidNET Alexa Skill

The SquidNET Alexa Skill is available from the Amazon store. Additionally, you can activate the skill from your Alexa device by saying, "Alexa, enable SquidNET". The SquidNET Alexa Skill provides information about map rotations, all sub and special weapons, and retrieves the latest tweets from both official and unnoficial Splatoon Twitter accounts.
